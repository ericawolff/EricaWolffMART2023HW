Venti

For this recipe, we will be using Venti measurements.

Step 1

Pump two pumps of Honey Blend syrup into the cup.

Step 2

Put one sachet of Peach Tranquility and one sachet of Jade Citrus Mint tea in the cup.

Step 3

Fill the cup halfway with hot water.

Step 4

Fill the rest of the cup with steamed lemonade.
