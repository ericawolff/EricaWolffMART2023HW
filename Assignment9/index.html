How to: Pump Gas

An Instructable by Erica Wolff

What is Needed

  • Car
  • Money
    • Cash
    • Credit

Step 1: Strategy

Find a gas station near you. You can use apps like GasBuddy to find cheaper prices.

Step 2: Preparation

Notice the side your gas tank is on by using the arrow picture, as seen below. Also note the kind of gas your vehicle takes; this can gnerally be found in your user manual.

Step 3: Beginning

Pull up to any available pump, with your gas tank opening as close as possible to the pump. Some vehicles have a lever or other mechanism to unlock the gas tank lid, while others can be opened from the outside.

IMPORTANT: Make sure you turn your vehicle off!

Step 4: Paying

Select your payment method and pay for your gas. You've got to pay for the gas before you select your variety of gas and pump it. You can pay with a credit or debit card directly at the pump, or with your card or cash at the counter inside.

Step 5: Pumping Gas

As mentioned, now you may select your gas and remove the gas nozzle from the holster. Take your gas cap off and insert the nozzle securely into the car. Pull the trigger, and if you'd like, lock it in place by flipping the lock on. If you want to fill your tank, wait for the pump to automatically stop. If not, when you are ready, squeeze the handle to release it from automatically flowing and remove it from your car and back into the holster. From here, screw your gas cap back on until it clicks a few times. Yay!! You did it!!!
